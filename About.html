About Us

Our mission

To gather, to grow and to inspire mechanical designers in SUTD

Our aim

We aim to gather mechanical designers with both passion and skills to collaborate on amazing and inconceivable projects that push beyond the limits of today.

What we do

We provide training workshops throughout the year to equip students with the two most widely used 3D modelling softwares in SUTD. Fusion 360 workshops are held in collaboration with trainers from Autodesk Singapore, while Solidworks workshops are held by our own members. These workshops equip students with the competencies to take on projects with confidence and produce their own digital prototype. We also provide certification examinations for the above mentioned softwares.

Apart from 3D modelling workshops, we also organize 3D modelling competition and DIY workshops that aim to allow members and students to hone their skills.
